About Maya Tours Guatemala

Our story, mission, and commitment to authentic experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate Guatemalan guides, Maya Tours Guatemala was born from a desire to share our country's incredible cultural and natural heritage with the world. What began as a small operation with just a few local tours has grown into a respected agency offering experiences throughout Guatemala.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing authentic, responsible, and unforgettable travel experiences that celebrate Guatemala's Maya heritage while supporting local communities. We believe tourism should be a force for good, preserving cultural traditions and protecting natural wonders for future generations.

Our Values

Authenticity

We prioritize genuine cultural interactions and experiences that go beyond surface-level tourism.

Sustainability

Our tours are designed to minimize environmental impact and support conservation efforts.

Community

We work directly with local communities to ensure they benefit fairly from tourism.

Expertise

Our guides are knowledgeable professionals passionate about sharing their heritage.

Our Team

Our team consists of local experts, including archaeologists, historians, naturalists, and indigenous community members who bring unique perspectives to our tours. Many of our guides are descendants of the Maya civilization they help interpret, providing insights you won't find anywhere else.

Safety & Responsibility

Your safety and comfort are our top priorities. All our tours adhere to strict safety standards, and our staff are trained in first aid and emergency procedures. We maintain small group sizes to ensure personalized attention and minimize environmental impact.

Certified Guides

All guides are licensed and receive regular training

24/7 Support

Emergency contact available at all times

Responsible Tourism

We follow global sustainable tourism standards
