Maya Tours Website Plan

Site Name

Maya Tours Guatemala

This name clearly identifies the business as a tour operator specializing in Guatemala with a focus on Maya culture. The name is simple, memorable, and directly communicates the company's purpose.

Optional domain availability: mayatoursgt.com or mayatoursguatemala.com

Site Purpose

The Maya Tours website will serve as the primary digital platform for:

  • Showcasing Guatemala's cultural, natural, and archaeological attractions
  • Providing detailed information about available tours and travel packages
  • Facilitating bookings and inquiries from potential customers
  • Educating visitors about Maya civilization and Guatemalan culture
  • Establishing the agency as a trusted expert in Guatemalan tourism

Scenarios

Target audience questions that will drive content:

  1. What are the most popular Maya archaeological sites I can visit with your tours, and what makes them special?
  2. Do you offer adventure packages that combine cultural experiences with activities like hiking or kayaking?
  3. What safety measures do you have in place for international travelers?
  4. Can I customize a private tour based on my specific interests and schedule?

Color Schema

The color palette is inspired by Guatemala's natural landscapes and Maya cultural elements:

Primary (#2E7D32)
Mayan Green
Secondary (#D4AF37)
Gold
Accent (#5D4037)
Earth Brown

Color Applications:

  • Primary (Mayan Green): Headers, navigation bar, buttons, primary call-to-action elements
  • Secondary (Gold): Accent borders, highlights, secondary buttons, special offers
  • Accent (Earth Brown): Footer, some section backgrounds, borders
  • Background: Light cream (#FFF8E1) for page background
  • Text: Dark gray (#212121) for body text

Typography

Font selections that complement the cultural theme while maintaining readability:

Playfair Display (Serif)

Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) to convey elegance and cultural heritage.

Montserrat (Sans-serif)

Used for body text, navigation, and all other content for optimal readability across devices.
